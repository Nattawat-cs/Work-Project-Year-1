A Traditional Family Favorite

About the Recipe:

preparation Time:15 mins

Cooking TIme::45 mins

Level of Difficulty:Easy

Number of servings:6
Ingredients:
  • 225g unsalted butter
  • 180g caster sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 225g self-raising flour
  • 1 lemon, zested
  • 2 lemons, juiced
  • 65g caster sugar
Recipe for the cake:
Step 1 Pre-heat the over to 180¬∞C
Step 2 Beat together the butter and caster sugar until creamy. Add the eggs one at a time,slowly mixing through
Step 3 Sift in the self-raising flour,then add the lemon zest and mix until well combined
Step 4 Line a loaf tin (8 x 21 cm) with greaseproof paper, then pour in the mixture and level the top with a spoon.
Step 5 Bake for 45 to 50 minutes.
Recipe for the lemon drizzle:
  1. Mix the lemon juice and caster sugar to make the drizzle.
  2. Prick the cake all over with a fork, then pour over the drizzle ‚Äì Let the juice sink in. The sugar will form a sweet, crisp topping.
  3. Leave the cake in the tin until completely cool before serving.
